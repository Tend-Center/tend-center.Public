About The Event

Men, Fire, and Something Way Bigger

Join the kickoff conversation and bonfire for men who are searching for something real in an increasingly artificial world. Men have lost their flame — what if in walking together, we could reignite it and keep it blazing?

  • What if you could be yourself?
  • What if walking with God could be meaningful and fun?
  • What would it be like to do life with men who know the real you and love you for it?
  • What if the balance of being husband, father, friend, and provider wasn’t so hard?
