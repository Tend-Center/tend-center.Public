Tend Center’s mission is to equip men to tend their flame.

We do this by walking with one another, building fires, and doing real things in an increasingly artificial world.

TENDING TO WHAT MATTERS

Let’s not overthink this.

We live in an overstimulated, overcommitted, and overly noisy time.

Despite all the information - I often feel ill-equipped.


Let’s quiet the noise.

Let’s get equipped.


Often, I’m too serious. I’m too cynical. This cocktail leads to isolation.

What’s the antidote? Fun actually. At least it starts with fun.

Let’s be men who do real things in an increasingly artificial time.


I don’t have all the answers - and neither do you.


Let’s stop outsourcing our meaningful conversations to popular podcasts. Merely watching friendship.

I want my own deep conversations. I want to walk with men. I want to honor God. I want to lead my family. I want to be held accountable to a standard. I want to grow but I also want to have fun.


Not prove myself, not fake it till I make it. Instead, live in authenticity.

If you want that too, click the link below, shoot me an email, and together we will forge a better path. Tend your flame.
